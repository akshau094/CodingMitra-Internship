## ğŸ¯ Key Findings

### ğŸ“Š **Content Insights**

1. **Movie Dominance**
   - Movies constitute approximately 69% of Netflix's catalog
   - TV Shows represent 31% of content
   - Movie additions have grown consistently

2. **Geographic Distribution**
   - US leads with most content production
   - India is second-largest content producer
   - Strong representation from UK and Asian markets

3. **Temporal Patterns**
   - Significant content addition surge from 2015-2020
   - Peak additions in recent years
   - Older content from 1940s-2000s still available

4. **Rating Analysis**
   - TV-MA (Mature Audience) most common rating
   - Strong focus on adult content
   - Diverse rating distribution for family content

5. **Duration Patterns**
   - Average movie duration: ~90-100 minutes
   - Standard deviation indicates variety
   - TV Shows typically 1-2 seasons

### ğŸŒ **Market-Specific Insights**

**Indian Content:**
- Rapidly growing segment
- Mix of Movies and TV Shows
- Diverse rating categories
- Regional language content increasing

**Correlation Findings:**
- Positive correlation between release year and addition year
- Trend towards adding recent content
- Catalog includes classic films
